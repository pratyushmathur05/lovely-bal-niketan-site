Our Story

Welcome to Lovey Bal Niketan

Lovey Bal Niketan School (LBN School) is nurturing a generation of problem solvers, progressive thinkers, and empathetic individuals who embrace technology while staying rooted in strong core values.

The school strives to achieve excellence in both academics and sports, giving equal importance to overall growth. Established in 1978 and affiliated with the MP Board, LBN School has been running successfully for many years.

The school has grown steadily and now offers education up to Class 12. It continues to set new milestones in holistic development by turning learning opportunities, challenges, and obstacles into pathways toward the highest levels of excellence.
